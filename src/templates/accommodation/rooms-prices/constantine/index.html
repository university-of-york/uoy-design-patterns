Constantine College

This college is located near the Sport Village on Campus East.

Constantine has larger beds (3/4 size doubles) with televisions in the shared kitchens. Blocks are organised in flats which provides both privacy and the opportunity to meet other people in the block.

Our wide range of small, special interest events create social networks, and you'll be encouraged to try new things. A focus on volunteering and enterprise will provide opportunities for you to develop skills and experience, and explore who you want to be. 

One of our students shared their experiences with us. Read their blog here.
