Alcuin College

Alcuin features all ensuite rooms in a mix of flats and houses positioned around grassy quads.

You can get a bite to eat at Alcuin Bistro café or nearby cosy café The Kitchen (run by the Students’ Union).

From college balls to fundraising activities, Alcuin members know how to balance work with play. They pride themselves on being enterprising and trying out new things.

Quick facts

  • 540 rooms
  • £132 - 135 per week
  • Standard ensuite and premium ensuite self-catered rooms
